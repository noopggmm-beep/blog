## 1. ⚛️ High-Purity Silicon-28 Achieves Mass Production — Breakthrough in Core Material for Silicon-Based Quantum Chips

The Nuclear Industry Physical and Chemical Engineering Research Institute of CNNC has achieved, for the first time, kilogram-scale independent mass production of silicon-28 isotopes with an abundance exceeding 99.99%, a dramatic improvement from the natural abundance of 92%. Hailed as "the purest silicon in the world," this is an indispensable core material for silicon-based quantum chips.

**Source**: Xinhua News Agency, CCTV News

## 2. 🔬 Quantum Spin Liquid "Spinons" — First Experimental Evidence Obtained

A team at University College Cork in Ireland developed "spin-witness spectroscopy" technology and obtained the first experimental evidence of the key quasiparticle "spinons" in herbertsmithite material, opening a new path for topological quantum computing.

**Source**: Xinhua News Agency

## 3. 🤖 AI Begins to Understand Physics — World's First General World Foundation Model Released

On June 12, the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI) released "Wujie·Physis-v0.1," marking an AI paradigm shift from "predicting the next token" to "predicting the next physical state," enabling understanding of time, space, and physical laws in the real world.

**Source**: Beijing Daily

## 4. 🚀 Zhuque-2 Improved Variant — Successful Double-Satellite Launch, Mass Production Begins

On June 10, the Zhuque-2 improved variant Yao-6 successfully launched two satellites. The rocket uses pneumatic push rods instead of traditional pyrotechnics for stage separation (enabling reusability) and 3D-printed engine mounts. The design is fully finalized, officially entering mass production mode.

**Source**: CCTV News

## 5. 💾 Japan Develops Laser-Write Magnetic Storage Material — Data Switching Speed Increased 1,000x

Japan's National Institutes for Quantum Science and Technology achieved "all-optical magnetic switching" in CoFeB systems, using femtosecond laser pulses to directly write data at speeds approximately 1,000 times faster than traditional current-driven memory.

**Source**: People's Daily

## 6. 💎 Wireless Chips Embedded with Single-Crystal Diamond Break Thermal Barrier

An MIT team embedded single-crystal diamond as a heat-spreading layer in gallium nitride chips, fabricating wireless power amplifiers with record-breaking performance, providing chip-level thermal management for 6G communications and satellite internet.

**Source**: Xinhua News Agency

## 7. 🔬 New High-Frequency Transistor Successfully Developed

The Institute of Metal Research of the Chinese Academy of Sciences developed a "silicon-graphene-germanium" barrier transistor, achieving a world-record current gain for transistors, offering new solutions for 6G and terahertz communications.

**Source**: CCTV News

## 8. 🌊 "Ocean Geology No. 6" Completes 10,000-Meter Deep-Sea Scientific Expedition

The vessel completed sea trials of a full-ocean-depth electromagnetic acquisition station, marking a major breakthrough in China's deep-sea electromagnetic measurement technology.

**Source**: Xinhua News Agency

## 9. ☀️ China's Largest Solar-Hydrogen-Storage Integrated Project Completed

The Rudong solar-hydrogen-storage integrated project in Jiangsu has been fully completed, with a total photovoltaic capacity of 400,000 kW, providing a demonstration model for clean energy comprehensive utilization.

**Source**: China Youth Daily

## 10. 🧠 World's First Multimodal Neuroscience Large Model Brainμ1.0 Released

BAAI released Brainμ1.0, alongside FlagOS 2.1 covering 32 chip models from 18 chip manufacturers, promoting collaborative development of AI software and hardware ecosystems.

**Source**: Beijing Daily
