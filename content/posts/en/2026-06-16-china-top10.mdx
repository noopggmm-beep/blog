## 1. 🚀 Eight Satellites Successfully Launched in a Single Rocket

On June 15, China used the Lijian-1 Yao-14 carrier rocket at the Dongfeng Commercial Space Innovation Test Zone to successfully launch 8 satellites, including the Jixing Gaofen 07C04 satellite, into orbit. This was the 14th flight of the Lijian-1 rocket.

**Source**: CCTV News

## 2. ⚛️ High-Purity Silicon-28 Mass Production Achieved — Quantum Chip Core Material Breakthrough

CNNC's Nuclear Industry Physical and Chemical Engineering Research Institute achieved, for the first time, kilogram-scale independent mass production of silicon-28 isotopes with over 99.99% purity. CAS Academician Yu Dapeng commented that this "paves the way for scalable qubit manipulation in silicon-based quantum computing."

**Source**: Xinhua News Agency, CCTV News

## 3. ⚡ Ningde Nuclear Power Unit 5 Dome Installation Completed

On June 15, the CGN "Hualong One" nuclear power unit transitioned from the civil construction phase to the equipment installation phase, marking significant progress in the construction of China's indigenous third-generation nuclear power technology.

**Source**: CCTV News

## 4. 🚢 World's Largest LNG Carrier Begins Construction

On June 9, the 271,000-cubic-meter ultra-large LNG carrier officially began construction, with delivery planned for 2028, setting a new global record for LNG carrier capacity.

**Source**: China Youth Daily

## 5. 🏗️ World's Longest-Span Steel Arch Bridge Completes Main Steel Structure

On June 12, the steel-concrete composite beams of the Fenglai Daxi River Bridge in Chongqing were joined, with a main span of 580 meters, setting a new world record for steel arch bridge span length.

**Source**: Xinhua News Agency

## 6. ✈️ C919 Deliveries Surpass 100 Aircraft

COMAC announced that cumulative C919 deliveries have surpassed 100 aircraft, with routes covering major domestic cities, marking a new phase in the commercial operation of China's domestically-produced large aircraft.

**Source**: CCTV News

## 7. 🌾 National Summer Grain Harvest Nears 90% Completion

As of June 15, 304 million mu of wheat had been harvested nationwide, with a bumper summer grain harvest in sight. The National Flood Control and Drought Relief Headquarters raised the flood response level for Guangdong and Guangxi to Level 3.

**Source**: Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Affairs

## 8. ⚖️ CCDI Reports Five Typical Cases of Misguided Performance Metrics

Pan Liang, former vice-ministerial-level official of the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission, was expelled from the Party. Shanghai Vice Mayor Chen Yujian is under disciplinary review and investigation. The "National Anti-Fraud in Action" campaign month was launched, with telecom fraud cases declining year-on-year for 8 consecutive months.

**Source**: Xinhua News Agency, China Court Network

## 9. 🏫 College Graduate Recruitment Fair Concludes

A total of 23,000 recruitment events were held both online and offline, providing 12.681 million job openings to assist college graduates in finding employment.

**Source**: Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security

## 10. 📄 China to Release White Paper on Global Governance

On June 17, the State Council Information Office will release the white paper "Building a More Just and Reasonable Global Governance System: China's Vision, Initiatives, and Actions." Xinhua News Agency will simultaneously release the think tank report "Chinese Youth: Strivers and Global Contributors in the New Era."

**Source**: Xinhua News Agency
