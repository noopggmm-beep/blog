## Welcome 👋

Hello, and welcome to my personal blog! After some preparation, this blog is finally live.

### Why Start a Blog?

There are several benefits to writing a blog:

1. **Knowledge Organization** — Systematically output what you've learned to deepen understanding
2. **Knowledge Archive** — Record problems encountered and solutions found in real projects
3. **Sharing and Discussion** — Exchange ideas with others and get feedback
4. **Personal Brand** — Long-term accumulation builds professional influence

### What Makes This Blog Special?

- 🚀 Built with Next.js, statically generated, blazing fast
- 🌙 Dark mode support to protect your eyes
- 📝 Posts written in Markdown, simple and efficient
- 🏷️ Tag-based categorization for easy browsing
- 📡 RSS feed support

### What I Plan to Write About

- **Tech Innovation**: Frontier technology trends and breakthroughs
- **IP Strategy**: Patent analysis, intellectual property law, and strategy
- **World News**: Top 10 daily global news with in-depth analysis
- **China News**: Top 10 daily domestic news highlights
